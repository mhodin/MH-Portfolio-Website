My Services

Identity / Branding

I have a lot of experience thus far with helping design a cohesive brand for companies ranging from a local sports academy to American University. My branding designs have included logos, business cards, stationaries, posters, advertisements, and more.

Merchandise Design

Having been in dozens of clubs and teams, I have been designing merchandise since freshman year of high school. I received the most experience in designing for wearables via the Alpha Epsilon Pi fraternity that I started at Penn State.

Music Design

Other than design, I am extremely passionate about music. I couldn't think of a better way to progress in my graphic design career then pursuing music design. I have made logos for bands as well as album covers, media/EPK kits, and posters.

Get To Know Me

My name is Matt Hodin. I am a 21 year old Graphic Design student, currently working towards my BFA in Graphic Design at The Maryland Institute College of Art (MICA) in Baltimore, MD. I grew up close to DC in North Potomac, MD. I studied three years in The Stuckeman School of Design at Penn State University, before transferring to MICA. I have been freelancing for the past two years, making logos, business cards, album covers, promotional material, and more. Some of my past clients include American University, Regnet Environmental Services, and Tribal Threadz, LLC. The past two summers, I have interned at Xanthus Design in DC and Hirshorn Zuckerman Design Group in Rockville, MD.

I found the field of graphic design as a freshman and it was truly love at first design. I really enjoy combining my artistic talents with the Adobe Suite to create cohesive and visually appealing designs that work. Email me at matt.hodin@gmail.com for possible work or for any other reason!
